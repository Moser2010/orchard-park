Family Owned

Local owners Virgal and Jan Carner, along with their daughter Susan Middleton RN run Orchard Park. Their family values flows into Orchard Park as they aspire to create a family environment. Over 25 years of experienced, Carner give excellent and tender care as if everyone is family. While an assisted living community, our residents enjoy a home setting that includes services and activities with pleasant associations.

Health Focused

We balance recreation and restoration to rebuild a child-like joy in our home. We aim to maintain and improve the resident wellness by incorporating fundamental health principles into everyday life. This includes fresh air, sunlight, exercise, adequate rest, a nutritious diet, abundant water, and trust in a Divine Power.
