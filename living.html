Living The Good Life

Gardens

We value the importance of exercise and nature. Our gardens provide a place to walk around in the summer. They provide a tranquil view for each resident. In the spring, flowers and small animals bring the gardens alive. Our garden is available for planting our resident’s own creations with raised pots for easy access.

Porch

Hang out with your friends and family on our porch. The comfortable chairs with sturdy armrest for support are perfect for a heated debate or the day’s gossip. The porch is covered to protect sensitive skin while providing fresh air.

Outdoor Dinning Area

The outdoor dinning area is perfect for family to come on by and celebrate a birthday or just catch up. With a birdhouse and flowers for entertainment and cement paths for safety everyone will have fun.

Living Room Area

Come to the living room for a change in environment. At any time, several residents may be on the comfortable couches chatting about their children. Take some time to tweet at the two small birds or watch the price is right with us. Or maybe read a good book while enjoying the sunlight from the big windows.

Small Bedroom

The small bedroom is perfect for a single resident. Residents bring their own family pictures and their favorite lamp to make it feel like home. It comes with cable TV for your entertainment and has a window overlooking the gardens. All rooms come with pull cords for safety.

Large Bedroom

A couple could share our large bedroom. Residents fill the spacious room with the comfort of a favorite chair or bookcase. The window views the gardens. Individual furnace/air conditioners regulate the temperature in each room. Pull cords protect our guests incase of a fall.

Bathroom

All bathrooms are equipped with high grade safety features. Pull cords that are easy to reach if help is needed. Strong hand holders line most walls to facilitate movement throughout the room. Plenty of space allows walkers and wheel chairs access.
