Activities

Group Orintated

Every day holds new excitement for our residents. Whether Monday Bingo or a New Years Eve party the activity room is the place to be. Our resident look forward to Tuesday for revelation of that weeks coloring contest winner. We also have monthly events including baking, sing-a-longs, nail care, birthday parties, and live entertainment.

Health Focused

Exercise and movement is important which is why we offer exercise to everyone twice a day. Residents love the big ball games. We offer Wii sports such as bowling. In the summer, we open the doors to gardening and daily group walks through our gardens.
